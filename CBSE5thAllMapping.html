LearnSMTH

CBSE Grade 5: The 360° Integrated Mission Map

The Grade 5 Differentiator: Adding Voice to Science

The Standard Approach

Students build a science model, solve arbitrary math problems, and write unrelated essays in English class. Subjects never talk to each other.

The LearnSMTH 360° Approach

Students don't just build a device. They quantify the data (Math), analyze the societal impact (EVS), and communicate their findings (English). We transform a "science project" into a full-scale professional challenge.

  • 100% NCERT EVS (Looking Around)
  • 100% NCERT Math-Magic
  • 100% NCERT English (Marigold)
  • 1 Major Hands-On Build / Month

The 10-Mission Curriculum

Month 01

01. The Ocean Economist

Real Problem How do local fishermen navigate the seas, track their catch, and run a sustainable business?
Workshop Build Magnetic compass navigation kit & boat buoyancy model.
EVS (Science/Social) Super Senses How marine animals navigate and communicate under the sea.
Math The Fish Tale Large numbers, place value, and basic economics (profit/loss of catch).
English (Marigold) Teamwork Collaborative writing: Planning the roles of a fishing crew.
Month 02

02. The Heritage Mapmaker

Real Problem How do we map ancient forts and ruins so modern tourists don't get lost?
Workshop Build 3D architectural diorama and scaled grid-mapping tools.
EVS (Science/Social) Walls Tell Stories Historical monuments, ancient architecture, and heritage preservation.
Math Mapping Your Way Scaling, grids, map reading, and calculating distances.
English (Marigold) Robinson Crusoe Descriptive writing: Creating a travelogue for a newly discovered ruin.
Month 03

03. Food Rescue Chemist

Real Problem How do we prevent tons of crops from spoiling during hot summers without electricity?
Workshop Build Passive solar dehydrator and natural preservation kit.
EVS (Science/Social) Mangoes Round the Year & Tasting/Digesting Food preservation techniques and human digestion.
Math Parts and Wholes Using fractions to mix precise preservation recipes (salt/sugar ratios).
English (Marigold) Wonderful Waste Instructional writing: Writing clear, step-by-step recipes.
Month 04

04. Outbreak Detective

Real Problem How do public health officials track and prevent mosquito-borne illnesses during monsoons?
Workshop Build Mosquito lifecycle observation trap and data tracking board.
EVS (Science/Social) A Treat for Mosquitoes Vector-borne diseases, stagnant water, and blood testing basics.
Math Smart Charts Using tally marks and bar graphs to track outbreak data hot-spots.
English (Marigold) The Little Bully Persuasive writing: Designing impactful public health campaign posters.
Month 05

05. Disaster Relief Architect

Real Problem How do we rapidly design and deploy sturdy shelters after an earthquake?
Workshop Build Earthquake shake-table and structural geometry kit.
EVS (Science/Social) When the Earth Shook Seismology, natural disasters, and emergency response.
Math Boxes and Sketches Translating 2D blueprints into 3D structural nets and perspectives.
English (Marigold) Gulliver's Travels News reporting: Writing a factual emergency broadcast script.
Month 06

06. Zero-Gravity Engineer

Real Problem How do you design living quarters and equipment for an international space station?
Workshop Build Centrifuge gravity simulator model and orbital mechanics rig.
EVS (Science/Social) Sunita in Space Gravity, vacuum, and surviving in low-earth orbit.
Math Tenths and Hundredths Using decimals for hyper-precise spacecraft measurements and mass.
English (Marigold) Flying Together Journaling: Writing the daily logs of an extended space mission.
Month 07

07. The Smart Agronomist

Real Problem How do we maximize crop yield in a very small plot of land to feed a community?
Workshop Build Seed germination grid and micro-greenhouse model.
EVS (Science/Social) Seeds and Seeds Plant biology, seed dispersal, and modern vs traditional farming.
Math Be My Multiple, I'll be Your Factor Using factors and arrays to optimize crop planting layouts.
English (Marigold) Who Will be Ningthou? Interviewing: Formulating questions for local farmers/community leaders.
Month 08

08. The Basecamp Leader

Real Problem How do mountaineers calculate safe climbing paths and survive extreme cold?
Workshop Build Inclinometer (angle measurer) and lung-capacity testing gauge.
EVS (Science/Social) Up You Go! & Blow Hot, Blow Cold High altitude survival, oxygen depletion, and heat transfer.
Math Shapes and Angles Calculating safe climbing trajectories and structural tent angles.
English (Marigold) Around the World Team Communication: Expressing empathy and leadership in writing.
Month 09

09. Energy Auditor

Real Problem How fast is our city running out of fossil fuels, and how do we switch to solar?
Workshop Build Solar panel surface area optimizer and basic energy circuit.
EVS (Science/Social) What if it Finishes? Fossil fuel depletion, traffic pollution, and renewable energy.
Math Area and its Boundary Calculating the total roof space required for solar panel arrays.
English (Marigold) Class Discussion Persuasive Debate: Structuring an argument for renewable energy adoption.
Month 10

10. Cultural Cryptographer

Real Problem How do different tribes and cultures use mathematics and geometry to tell stories?
Workshop Build Tessellation block-printing kit and geometric loom model.
EVS (Science/Social) Whose Forests? Tribal cultures, forest conservation, and cultural heritage.
Math Can You See the Pattern? Symmetry, reflections, and tessellations in traditional textiles.
English (Marigold) Malu Bhalu Creative Writing: Writing poetry to describe abstract geometric patterns.
