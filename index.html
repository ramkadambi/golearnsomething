What Happens Inside a 3-Hour Mission?

Every "Learn Something" session is designed like a cinematic engineering adventure where children learn by building, experimenting, collaborating, and presenting.

  • 1. Mission Briefing Students receive a high-stakes challenge like protecting a museum vault or saving a smart greenhouse.
  • 2. Discovery Through Play Children explore sensors, LEDs, buzzers, motors, and circuits through guided experimentation.
  • 3. Team Prototype Build Small teams collaborate on breadboards to test ideas, troubleshoot problems, and improve designs.
  • 4. Individual PCB Build Every student assembles their own custom STEM project using beginner-friendly PCB kits.
  • 5. Mission Test Arena Students test their creations in real mission scenarios filled with excitement and friendly competition.
  • 6. Show & Tell Presentation Each child explains their project, reinforcing learning while building communication confidence.
