LearnSMTH

Grade 6 Science Mission Map

Where LearnSMTH Becomes Differentiated

Most STEM companies start with:

"What project can we build?"

We start with:

"What chapter are students struggling to connect with real life?"

Then we convert every chapter into a mission.

  • 1 Major Workshop Mission / Month
  • 3 Weeks of Online Learning
  • Online Modules Connect to Chapters
  • Same Mission Universe & Characters
  • Creates Continuity & Reduces Parent Overload

The Core Differentiator Pipeline
