Mission: Build a Mars Rover

Real Problem: How do scientists build machines to navigate, explore, and measure terrain on other planets without being there?

Grade 3
The Hardware Build
Rubber-band powered mechanical rover with basic chassis.
NCERT EVS / Science
Left-Right (Directions) Moving Things

Understanding heavy vs. light materials, and mapping basic directional movement.

NCERT Math
Where to Look From Fun with Numbers

Top/side/front views of the rover. Measuring distances in "blocks" or handspans.

Grade 4
The Hardware Build
Battery & motor-powered rover with a simple push-button switch.
NCERT EVS / Science
Friction & Force Basic Energy

Why wheels need grip (friction) and converting battery energy to motion.

NCERT Math
Halves and Quarters Tick-Tick-Tick

Timing the rover's speed (Time) and calculating halfway/quarter checkpoints.

Grade 5
The Hardware Build
Motorized rover with basic suspension and steering mechanics.
NCERT EVS / Science
Sunita in Space Gravity Concepts

How gravity changes on different planets and designing for uneven terrain.

NCERT Math
Area and its Boundary

Calculating the exact perimeter (boundary) the rover patrols on the mission map.

Grade 6
The Hardware Build
Electronic rover with breadboard, wheel encoders, and complex circuitry.
NCERT Science
Measurement & Units Motion Types

Rectilinear vs circular motion, and standardizing scientific units of distance.

NCERT Math
Mensuration Decimals

Advanced area calculations, using decimals for precise distance tracking via wheel rotations.

Mission: Smart Plant Rescue

Real Problem: Millions of plants die indoors because humans forget to water them or over-water them. Can we automate agriculture?

Grade 3
The Hardware Build
DIY Self-watering planter using capillary action (cotton wicks).
NCERT EVS / Science
The Plant Fairy

Identifying different parts of the plant, leaves, stems, and roots.

NCERT Math
Jugs and Mugs

Measuring water capacity. How many "mugs" of water does the reservoir hold?

Grade 4
The Hardware Build
Sunlight shadow tracker + engineered drip irrigation model.
NCERT EVS / Science
Roots and Flowers

Understanding how roots absorb water and the role of sunlight.

NCERT Math
Tick-Tick-Tick Tables & Shares

Building a precise watering schedule across the week using time and basic division.

Grade 5
The Hardware Build
Soil testing kit using color-changing pH & moisture chemical strips.
NCERT EVS / Science
A Seed Tells a Farmer's Story

Germination, agricultural practices, and crop yield optimization.

NCERT Math
Tenths and Hundredths Smart Charts

Measuring plant growth down to the millimeter and charting growth rates over 3 weeks.

Grade 6
The Hardware Build
Electronic soil moisture sensor connected to breadboard and buzzer.
NCERT Science
Plant Life Processes Conductors/Insulators

How water conducts electricity to close the circuit in the soil moisture detector.

NCERT Math
Introduction to Algebra

Writing variables and logic rules: If Moisture (M) < Threshold (X), then trigger Buzzer (Y).
