LearnSMTH

Grade 6 Interdisciplinary STEM Mission Map

Where LearnSMTH Becomes Differentiated

Most schools & programs teach in silos:

"Now it's time for Math."
"Now it's time for Science."

We start with the real world:

"What is a human problem we need to solve?"

When students solve missions, they naturally learn both the Science mechanism and the Math calculation required to make it work. No silos.

  • 1 Major Workshop Mission / Month
  • 3 Weeks of Online Learning
  • Same Mission Universe & Characters
  • 100% NCERT Math Embedded
  • 100% NCERT Science Embedded
